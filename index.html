About event

The Technology Start-up Career Fair is hosted by the Information & Computer Science Student Council (ICSSC) at UCI. The event will take place on April 25th from 1-5pm at the Student Center in Pacific Ballroom D.

The purpose of the Start-up Career Fair is to expose students to new and upcoming businesses in the technology sector. As students in the school of ICS, we are on the edge of new technologies and new ideas. Talking to these companies could land you your next internship, job or even CEO position!
